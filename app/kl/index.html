Kuala Lumpur Event

💍 Overview 💍

Event in KL will be a straigh forward simple evening reception, but lots of amazing food ready to wet your appetite. We all know far too well about the famous Malaysian timing, so keep it respectable and try to come on time so we don't have to delay and make everyone too hungry before everything. For the specific event location and time, more info down below. Just keep scrolling, we've even throw in a map for you!

👔 Dress code 👗

Come dress smart, come dress fun, come dress in something that you will be happy to be on photos. Keep it neat and tidy!

💵 Ang Pao / Gift registry 🎁

Traditionally within our culture, we didn't practice much of the gift registry part but instead, we do the 'Ang Pao' (translated as red packet in Hokkien/Teochew) as a token which consist of cash. It's a more practical approach for us since we are travelling light. Gifts are still welcomed, but keep it small and light.

Feel free to give in any currency you want, but keeping it local will be easier for us to handle. Just ask during the Evening reception where to drop it, we should have an dedicated spot prepared. For those who travel to 2 receptions, feel free to give in anyway you want, split it in 2 sides or drop all of them in 1. No pressure for everyone! 😁
